CONTENT

A gym offers a variety of equipment and facilities for physical exercise, including weight machines, free weights, cardio machines, and space for functional training. Members can participate in group fitness classes, such as yoga, spin, or aerobics, and may also receive guidance from personal trainers. Gyms aim to enhance fitness, strength, endurance, and overall well-being.

WORKOUT

A workout is a session of physical exercise designed to improve fitness, strength, and overall health. It can include activities like weightlifting, cardio, and flexibility exercises, tailored to individual goals and performed regularly for optimal results.

DIET

A diet is the sum of food and drink consumed by a person regularly. It can be tailored to specific health goals, such as weight loss or muscle gain, and often involves balanced nutrition, portion control, and mindful eating habits.
